About Naruto

Naruto Uzumaki is the main character of the Naruto manga series created by Masashi Kishimoto. He is a ninja from the Hidden Leaf Village who dreams of becoming the Hokage, the village leader. At the start of the series, Naruto is a young boy who is ostracized by the villagers because he has the Nine-Tailed Demon Fox sealed inside him. Throughout the series, Naruto works hard to gain the villagers' acknowledgment and achieve his dream of becoming Hokage.
